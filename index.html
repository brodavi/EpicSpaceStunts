Epic Space Stunts

Epic Space Stunts is based on the game of Asteroids. When I played Asteroids as a kid, I always liked narrowly dodging asteroids more than shooting them. I would put myself in harrowing situations, and onlookers would cheer when I somehow avoided crashing. I wanted to capture that alternative game mechanic, and offer it as the main goal of the game.

I hope you enjoy Epic Space Stunts!
